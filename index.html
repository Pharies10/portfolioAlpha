Background

I am a senior computer science major at Centre College in Danville, KY. While I might be studying in Kentucky, my hometown is San Diego, CA. At Centre, I have to balance my major with my athletic passion of lacrosse. As a D3 athlete, I have recieved multiple rewards for academic excellence and am one of the leading GPAs of the team.

My love of computer science came from the problem solving aspect of it. Being able to work out a challenging provlem and construct a tool in order to solve the problem has always been exciting for me. If my athletic career has taught me anything, it is that there is not a wall to high that I can't climb with a little hard work and determination. For every computer science problem, I approach it with the same stubborn determination for success that I have in the field.
